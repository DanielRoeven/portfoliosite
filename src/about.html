About

My name is Daniel Roeven and I am an interaction designer currently pursuing a masters degree in interaction design at Chalmers University of Technology in Gothenburg, Sweden. Before this, I got a bachelors degree in computer science at Radboud University Nijmegen and a minor in industrial design at Eindhoven University of Technology, both in the Netherlands.

Shaping technology as a tool for people to realise their ambitions is what excites me. I love creating products in interdisciplinary teams from inception to deployment. I have previously worked as an interaction designer, front-end developer, and teaching assistant (in a usability course).

My design skills stretch the whole spectrum: from user research, idea and concept generation, through sketching and prototyping, to evaluation and supporting implementation. The right process ensures a high-quality product, happy users, and the right decisions for the business and client.
