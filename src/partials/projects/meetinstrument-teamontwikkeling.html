About

I designed and developed a web app for a collaboration between Werken met Succes and Radboud University. The tool invites members of a team to fill out a questionnaire on their team. Upon completion, the tool produces a neat report displaying the teamâ€™s competences and areas it can grow in.

I designed the user experience for this tool, the interface (email and web), and developed and deployed it in JavaScript (with Meteor). It is a responsive, mobile-friendly web app (user research indicated that many participants completed it in public transit). Accessibility was a primary design guideline (colour-blind and screen reader friendly, for example).
