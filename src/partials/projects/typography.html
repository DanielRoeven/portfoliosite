Typographic Work

About

I believe that typography is fundamental to any good user experience. Done well, it conveys style and character, while gently guiding the user through the experience.

For this reason, I enjoy typesetting websites or apps, but also documents and books. I shape the page and set top-notch type: readable, beautiful, and in harmony with its content. I like working with both traditional paper and ink but also with pixels and screens. Shown above are some theses I've typeset, and handmade business cards I've made.

The letterpress business cards were printed on a proofing press at Mizdruk's workshop, using a custom developed photopolymer plate. If you're interested in the design process of the logotype you see, you can read more here.
