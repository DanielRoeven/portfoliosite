Portal for Radboud Centre for Social Sciences

About

I designed an online portal for the Radboud Centre for Social Sciences. The portal unifies information from varying services and provides clear overview and access.

I designed the interface and user experience and developed the front-end in React. I worked as the interaction designer alongside two developers in a team of three.

Existing user research indicated that users felt overwhelmed by the amount of services and information in the previous system. Yet simultaneously, users wanted to know what's happening in other parts of the organisation at a glance. This was further validated by us through interviews and questionnaires.

We addressed this by creating a portal which unifies the information flows of varying sources and hierarchies. In this portal users can select which information to view by relevancy: personal, team-wide, or organisation wide. Explicitly marking the information flows by color and group, and toggling them on or off gives users the best of both worlds: quick access to specific information, and an overview of the whole when desired. Finally, the portal also serves as a springboard to specific services, through single sign-on.

The interface was validated through "thinking out loud" evaluations. This showed that users of the new interface easily understood and manipulated the information.

Meetinstrument Teamontwikkeling

About

I designed and developed a web app for a collaboration between Werken met Succes and Radboud University. The tool invites members of a team to fill out a questionnaire on their team. Upon completion, the tool produces a neat report displaying the team’s competences and areas it can grow in.

I designed the user experience for this tool, the interface (email and web), and developed and deployed it in JavaScript (with Meteor). It is a responsive, mobile-friendly web app (user research indicated that many participants completed it in public transit). Accessibility was a primary design guideline (colour-blind and screen reader friendly, for example).

IntelliGUI

About

IntelliGUI is a web front-end which automatically generates and configures user interfaces.

Part internship and part university course, this project was carried out in a unique combination of both a student-driven company and a course for the Computer Science Bachelor at the Radboud University.

I worked in a team of eight, where I was responsible for designing and implementing a modular user interface using Angular.

The front-end solution we created is powered by CGI Arnhem's back-end. The resulting product allows for quick and flexible user interface generation and configuration based on requirements that the user inputs in a simple specification language.

Bachelor Thesis in Human-Computer Interaction

About

I wrote a Bachelor thesis in the field of Human-Computer Interaction. In it, I explore whether WYSIWYG word processors (such as Microsoft Word) can be improved by taking cues from the world of structured documents (such as LaTeX). Structured documents offer great advantages over WYSIWYG, but are renowned to be hard-to-use.

Through user observations, moderated task tests, and thinking-aloud tests, I identified areas in which the WYSIWYG processors could be improved, such as consistency in formatting, and automatic table-of-contents or bibliography generation. These are areas in which structured documents shine. Informed by user research and backed by HCI literature, I iteratively prototyped and evaluated a processor which enhances the WYSIWYG paradigm with benefits from structured documents.

Icon Design

About

Icons are the one of the first things users see when interacting with digital products. And every interaction after that, the icon is seen again, and again. Good icons have to be meaningful, memorable, visually pleasing, and long-lasting.

For this reason, I particularly enjoy handcrafting beautiful icons. I like how designing icons is an endeavour that combines design insight and artistic and technical skill.

I usually draw icons by hand with markers on paper, create a 3D model of it in Cinema4D, and then touch up the rendering in Photoshop. Some works in progress can be found on my instagram.

Typographic Work

About

I believe that typography is fundamental to any good user experience. Done well, it conveys style and character, while gently guiding the user through the experience.

For this reason, I enjoy typesetting websites or apps, but also documents and books. I shape the page and set top-notch type: readable, beautiful, and in harmony with its content.
